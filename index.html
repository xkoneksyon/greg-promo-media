The story

Built from Pétion-Ville. Amplified across the diaspora.

Greg Promo is a Haitian blogger, promoter, and community connector known for rapid cultural coverage, entertainment visibility, and events that bring Haitian artists, influencers, entrepreneurs, and fans into one space.

From his early social-media promotion work to large-scale community events in Miami, the brand has grown into a trusted platform for culture, announcements, music, sports, and positive youth engagement.
